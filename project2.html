A 6bit digital to analog converter is generated. While the input is a digital 6bit word, the analogue output is represented by a variable current.

Project Overview

  1. Understand the design and principle of a Digital to Analog Converter (DAC).
  2. Generate a hierarchical schematic.
  3. Verify the functionality by simulation.
  4. Draw a compact hierarchical layout that is LVS clean and free of DRC errors.
  5. Run a parasitic extraction and verify functionality with parasitics.
